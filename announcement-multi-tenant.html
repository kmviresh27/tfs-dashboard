⚠ The Problem We Solved
  • TFS queries maintained manually per team — no automated rollup or unified PI view

  • No isolation between teams — shared config meant no per-team branding, roles, or policies

Solution
🏢 Multi-Tenant 🔒 Dept Isolation 👤 Role-Based Access ⚡ Live 30-min Refresh 🔗 TFS + Azure DevOps 📊 PI / Sprint Tracking

Department Isolation
Each department gets its own config, connection, branding, custom roles, and data — completely separated. No cross-department data leakage.

Role & Visibility Policies

Dual Backend Support
